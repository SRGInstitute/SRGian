About Us

Our Mission

At SRG Computer Institute, we help students learn important computer skills that are useful in today's world. We offer courses on things like creating websites, developing apps, using Excel, learning Tally for accounting, graphic designing, and basic computer skills. Our goal is to teach you not just theory but also practical skills that you can use in real-life situations. Our experienced teachers make learning fun and easy, using simple methods that help you understand even the most difficult topics.

Our mission at SRG Computer Institute is to teach students important computer skills in a simple and easy way. We want to help students become skilled and confident in using technology for their future jobs or studies.
